WHAT WE DO

WHAT WE DO

Based on blockchain, MLC redefines offline media ecology and digital ads industry

What we provide

Using consumption facilities as the entry point, MLC integrates and utilizes idle or low utilization media screens such as TV, advertisement machine, LED, projector. By connecting to the intelligent trust device, each old media screen transforms into a new interactive digital ads player. All players are aggregated within MLC ecosystem as the media matrix from which a more accurate distribution and communication channel arises

Operations and processes

Interactive digital ads, relying on network, is a new model of ads. Audiences in-depth join in ads scene by connecting their mobiles to ads media screens. Using the operation system on mobile such as touch control, sensor or AR recognition, audiences receive various interesting experience in the process of ads display. Consequently, it greatly inspires the audience's participation which achieves the core value of ads + marketing.

Project information

MLC innovates the ads effect measurement by using IMC token as the basic unit. The properties of MLC token including source, flow, timestamp and message are all stored in chain block. Combining the analyze of these properties and the tag of consumption facilities such as industry, location and history data, MLC profiles an accurate portrait for every audience to guide the ads delivery.

Sectors we work in

MLC brings the first dapp, which is called online advertising trading platform. The dapp can show media screens, publish and integrate advertisement, choose and purchase by advertiser themselves, custom ads strategy. And it also imports third parts which can provide ads contents, data monitoring or management proxy service to perfect the trading chain. Advertisers can use the dapp to trace the effect of their ads and adjust the strategy at any time.
